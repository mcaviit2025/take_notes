// FAQs
Common Questions
Do I need coding knowledge to use my portfolio? +
Not at all! We deliver the complete code along with a simple guide on how to update your content (like changing your name, adding a new project). You just edit a few lines of text — no coding needed.
How do I make my portfolio live on the internet? +
We guide you through deploying it for free using GitHub Pages or Netlify. For Premium plan users, we handle the deployment for you. A custom domain (like yourname.com) costs around ₹500/year extra.
What information do I need to provide? +
We'll ask for: your resume, a photo (optional), project details (title, description, GitHub/live link), skills, education, and social links. We'll send you a simple form to fill out — no hassle.
What if I'm not happy with the design? +
Every plan includes revisions (1–3 depending on the plan). We share a preview first, take your feedback, and make changes before delivering the final version. Your satisfaction is the priority.
Is this only for MCA students? +
No! While StudyHub focuses on MCA and MBA, the portfolio service is open to any student — BCA, B.Tech, B.Com, MBA, or anyone who wants a professional online presence.
How do I pay? Is it safe? +
Payment is via UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) after we discuss your requirements and you're satisfied with the plan. We follow a simple: 50% advance → work starts → 50% on delivery process.
